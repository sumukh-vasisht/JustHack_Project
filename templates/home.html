UPDOWN

'UPDOWN' is the brain child of
three budding engineering students
from The National Institute of Engineering, Mysuru,
Varun Bheemaiah, Sumukh Vasisht S, Syed Amir.

'UP(load)DOWN(load)' is a document sharing
and storing facility aimed at
the sharing of subject materials

Gone are the days of begging for notes on Whatsapp
and other social media. Never again lose your notes
due to accidental deletions.
Just upload your notes and
download others' uploaded notes on the go!
